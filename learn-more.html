Our Handmade Crafts

At Dorbao, we take pride in offering a diverse range of handmade crafts, each created with care and passion. Our main products include Bearbrick, Tufting, and Bracelet DIY kits. Learn more about each of these unique crafts below.

Bearbrick

Our Bearbrick kits allow you to unleash your creativity. You can use paints to color your Bearbrick and choose your favorite colors to make it truly unique. Each Bearbrick is a blank canvas waiting for your artistic touch.

Tufting

With our Tufting kits, you can create your own custom rugs. Choose the pattern you want, and we'll project it for you. You can also select your favorite color scheme, and we'll trim the rug for you when you're done. It's a fun and rewarding way to make something special for your home.

Bracelet DIY

Our Bracelet DIY kits let you design and create your own bracelets. Choose from a variety of materials and styles to craft a bracelet that reflects your personal taste. If you need help, our staff is always ready to assist you in bringing your vision to life.
